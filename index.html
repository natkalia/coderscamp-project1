projects

These are projects ideas that I am currently working on:

Onboarding clients

For many professions it is important to understand who the business client is. My idea is a bot that uses public APIs to aggregate different scattered information about the client based only on his registration number.

Schedule your travel

A friend of mine is passionate traveller and perfectionist when it comes to organizing travel schedules. I want to help her create place in web, where she could use her talents to organize travels for others.

The company website of my parents family business is rather old-school. I want to update it so that it looks more modern, even if still conservative. I also want make it possible to make blog posts by the employees.

Medical bot

Sometimes it is difficult to get to see your doctor when you or your child need it. Instead of spending time on clicking through medical app and checking many times a day if there is any free place, I created a bot that is doing this for me. (The link to final code soon to come.)
